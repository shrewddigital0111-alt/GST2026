About GABANI Shark Tank 2026

GABANI Shark Tank 2026 is a student-driven platform designed to help young entrepreneurs present their ideas in a real, but supportive pitch environment. The goal is to:

  • Showcase Student Innovation
    From business concepts to social impact ideas, every original thought gets a stage.
  • Connect with Investors & Mentors
    Selected guests, including a Shark Tank India–experienced guest, will share honest feedback and guidance.
  • Build Real Skills
    Public speaking, pitching, financial thinking, teamwork and execution planning — all in one event.

Structure, rules & participant information

  • Who can participate?
    Open to students with an idea, prototype, business model or concept — solo or team (max 4 members).
  • Pitch Format
    3–5 minutes pitch + 5 minutes Q&A with the panel. Visual slides are allowed (bring on USB or share in advance).
  • Selection Process
    Shortlisting will be based on your Google Form responses and idea clarity. Final list of teams will be shared before event day.
  • Awards & Recognition
    Best Pitch, Best Innovation, High Potential Idea & Audience Choice, plus certificates for all shortlisted teams.
